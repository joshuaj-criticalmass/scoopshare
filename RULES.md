# ScoopShare Rules

## Goal

Build an ice cream cone with 3 scoops of the same flavor.

## How the Game Starts

1. Join the game on your phone by entering your name.
2. Wait in the lobby for the host to start the round.
3. When the game begins, you will get a cone with 3 random scoops.

Duplicates are allowed, so you may start close to a winning cone.

## How to Play

1. Walk around the room and talk to other players.
2. Find someone who has a flavor you want.
3. Open the trade screen and choose:
   - who you want to trade with
   - which scoop you are offering
   - which flavor you want back
4. The other player receives your trade request and can accept or decline.

If they accept, the swap happens instantly for both players.

## Trade Rules

- You can only offer a scoop you actually have.
- You can ask for any flavor, but if the other player does not currently have it, your trade will fail.
- If your request is declined, you must wait 5 seconds before sending another trade.
- If you ask for a flavor the other player does not have, you must also wait 5 seconds before sending another trade.

This cooldown is there to keep the game focused on real conversations, not spam requests.

## Winning

- You win as soon as all 3 of your scoops match.
- Winning places are awarded in order: first, second, third, and so on.
- The game can keep going after the first winner so more players can place.
- The host ends the round when it is time to stop.

## Quick Tips

- Talk before you send a trade.
- Use the flavor guide on screen if you forget a flavor name.
- Move quickly, because another player may trade away the scoop you want before your request is accepted.

## Simplified Rules

- Click the QR code and enter your name.
- Wait for the host to start the game.
- You will get a cone with 3 different scoops of ice cream.
- Trade with other players to make all of your scoops the same flavour.
    If you request a flavour someone doesn't have or they reject your trade, you get a 5 second timeout.
- First, second, and third place are awarded in order.
